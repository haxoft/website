    12th until 18th October '16

    We were about to start our project "Planspiel Web Engineering", which we further will call planspiel. We teamed us in the first meeting of the planspiel and connected us over WhatsApp. Our first tasks was to give us a name and a logo. Easier said, than accomplished. We had many discussions about the name and it wasn't easy to find a name that was cool for all of us three. Finally in nearby the end of the week, we decided to name ourselfs 'Haxoft'. The merging of the words Hacking, as developing of really good code, and Software, as what we will deliver to our customers, was an accepted base of us all. As a funfact, the name "haxoft" was one of the first ideas we had. So we can say we took one of our most spontanious ideas for our name. Which will hopefully inspire us all over all the time. What we did not mentiond until now, was that we also had to give our company directly an own website. Therefore we serached for a bootstrap template to have a nice look and feel at our website from the very first beginbing of its 'life'. We were pretty fast on this decision and decided to take the template BRUSHED by Alessio Atzeni. Whereby Luis and Frank started to deliver our website to the internet, Valentin started to learn how he can create a logo for Haxoft. With inkscape and a few good youtube tutorials he created our known x-cube as the visualization of our company. At least he developed a first version. Which we all agreed on had to improve by the next week.
