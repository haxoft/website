    19th until 25th October '16

    The week started somehow very positve for us, due to the presentations of the tasks. In the planspiel meeting at wednesday, every team showed what they got until now. Of course this was not that much, but as a team we could say that we were very happy with our result and in comparison to the other teams we agreed that we love our page more than any other team's page. So this was a great push for the internal team mood. As a next step for the week, we should create values for our company. After everyone did some first research and thinking about what we want stand for, we met at friday morning to bring our ideas together. Within this meeting we agreed on 8 core values our company Haxoft should include. After writing them all down to one sheet of paper, we saw that ranking them could force them to be more or less important. But as core values there should no value be more or less important than another one. So ranking values or somehow write them in a list is not a way we want to deliver them. Therefore we decided to create a circle of our 8 core values. With such a circle no ranking can influence them. Not even subconscious. To have still a nice look and feel at our page we also included a description box below the circle to have only icons in the circle itself. By making each icon in the circle clickable and only showing only one active value description in the box below, every user can experience our values on his own. Moreover the circle is with only being made out of icons not that big and gives the user a nice play around possibilty while getting more information about us. The meeting on friday of course only ended with a short summary of our 8 values and was not a final version, but a first draft. Hence also the formulating of the final version gave us some discussion to fulfill every ones thoughts and ideas behind every value. Moreover this week showed us, that using WhatsApp as a communication channel between us three is not perfect. Of course we can chat there all together in a group, but the different dimensions of tasks and challenges we came to, were such different that one timeline of chats or one room for chats was not anymore effective. Most times each next step in each domain needs a short discussion or mind connecting. And we saw that this would end after week, but will continue in the future. In addition the domains will also move apart in progress, thus every domain will be more and more on its seperated from the other ones. Hence we needed a new tool for communicating remotely between us all. Then we remembered Prof. Gaedke talking about the awesome tool slack. We did not used it before, but we wanted to give it a try. So we created our own slack team and started to begin different channels for all different purposes we have. This brought us directly a better overview over the whole situation we are in and improved our communication. Moreover we worked on the improvement of our logo, as last week mentioned. The colors were a bit to bright and also some details were not fitting perfectly.
